About Me

I am currently a Ph.D.student in Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Hongsheng Li. Before that,I received my Bachelor's degree in Electric Engineering from Universitat Politecnica de Calunya, BarcelonaTech in 2015. My Chinese name is 潘俊廷.

I worked in Image Processing Group (GPI), advised by Prof. Xavier Giro since Spring 2015 and I have also collaborated with Prof. Kevin McGuiness from Dublin City University (DCU) and Dr. Cristian Canton from Facebook.

In Spring 2017, I visited Digital Video and MultiMedia Lab (DVMM Lab), advised by Prof. Shih-Fu Chang.

My research interests lie in computer vision, deep learning, and their applications. Specifically on tasks related to Video understanding, Video Generation, and Scene Understanding.
